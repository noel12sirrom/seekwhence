How To Play

Each round, the game highlights a sequence of tiles that you need to remember.

If you click the tiles in the correct order, an extra tile is added to the sequence and you move on to the next round. If you click the wrong tile at any point, the game is over, and your score is based on the number of rounds you completed.

See how long you can last as the sequence gets longer and more challenging!
